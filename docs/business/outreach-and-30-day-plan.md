## 7. 30-Day "First Revenue" Action Plan

**Goal:** At least one signed, manually-invoiced cohort pilot (~$20/seat/month) by Day 30.
**No billing system needed:** payment = a PDF invoice (free invoice generator) + bank transfer or a payment link.
**Core daily rhythm (every weekday):** 10 new outreach touches + reply to all inbound within 2 hours + post or prep 1 piece of video content.

### Week 1 — Build the Machine (Days 1–7)
- **Day 1:** Write your target list — 40 bootcamp directors/program leads + 40 corporate L&D/AI-upskilling leads on LinkedIn. Names, roles, links in a sheet.
- **Day 2:** Record + publish the 60-second demo video (§3). Pin it. Every email links to it.
- **Day 3:** Build a one-page pilot offer PDF: what Lumi is, demo link, pricing ($20/seat/mo), pilot scope (1 cohort, 30 days), how to start. Create a reusable invoice template.
- **Day 4:** Send Cold Email 1 to 10 bootcamp leads. DM 5 on LinkedIn. Log everything.
- **Day 5:** Send Cold Email 2 to 10 corporate L&D leads. DM 5. Post short-form Script A.
- **Day 6 (light):** Send all Day-4 follow-ups. Refill the list to keep 80+ prospects warm.
- **Day 7 (light):** Review which subject lines got opens/replies. Pick the winner. Batch-record 3 short videos.

**Week 1 targets:** 20 cold emails, 10 DMs sent, demo + offer PDF live, 1 short video posted.

### Week 2 — Volume + First Calls (Days 8–14)
- **Daily (8–12):** DM 10 leads/day + send 5 cold emails/day. Send all follow-ups due. Post 1 short video on Day 9 (Script B) and Day 12 (Script C).
- **Goal:** Book 3–5 discovery calls. On each: show the demo live, ask "would one cohort want this?", propose the 30-day pilot, name the price out loud.
- **Day 13:** Send a recap + pilot PDF to everyone who took a call. Ask: "Can we run a pilot with your next cohort?"
- **Day 14 (light):** Tighten the pitch based on objections heard.

**Week 2 targets:** ~50 DMs, ~25 emails, 3–5 calls booked, 2 short videos posted.

### Week 3 — Push to Commitment (Days 15–21)
- **Daily:** Keep 10 touches/day, but prioritize warm replies. Chase every "maybe."
- **Day 15–17:** Run pilot-proposal calls. Send the pilot PDF within 1 hour of each. Offer a concrete start date + a small "founding cohort" perk (locked $20 rate + your direct support).
- **Day 18:** For the hesitant, offer a **free 1-week mini-pilot for 5 seats** to de-risk — with a written plan to convert to paid.
- **Day 19–20:** Send pilot agreements (a simple 1-page email-confirmable scope + price). Get a verbal/email "yes."
- **Day 21 (light):** Prep the invoice template with real seat counts.

**Week 3 targets:** 2–3 verbal pilot commitments, at least 1 mini-pilot running.

### Week 4 — Close + Invoice (Days 22–30)
- **Day 22–24:** Convert commitments. Email: "Great — to lock it in, here's the invoice for [X seats × $20]. We start [date]." Send the manual invoice PDF.
- **Day 25:** Onboard the first paid cohort: setup steps (inline plugin on their Claude subs), a 1-pager for students, your contact for support.
- **Day 26–28:** Keep outreach at 10/day to build next month's pipeline. Ask each new pilot for a testimonial.
- **Day 29:** Follow up on unpaid invoices. Confirm payment received.
- **Day 30:** Review numbers: touches → replies → calls → pilots → revenue. Double down on the channel that produced the signed pilot.

**Week 4 targets:** **≥1 signed, invoiced, paid pilot.** Pipeline of 2+ pilots for next month. 2+ testimonials requested.
